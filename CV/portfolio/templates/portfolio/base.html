My Process

Research

Before the first pixel is created I do extensive research on your business, your industry, and your competition. I look for new opportunities for growth and creative ways to set yourself apart online.

Plan

What I learned in the research phase is put into a plan of action. It is here where we establish what exactly we are trying to accomplish with the website, and how we are going to get there.

Design

The design phase focuses on putting your plan into action by creating visual designs and interesting copy that conveys your message and differentiates you from your competition.

Build

Everything from the previous three phases comes together and I build your site. You provide feedback throughout this process and we make sure everything is just right.

Launch

Once we are sure everything is working properly and you're happy with your new website, we launch it to the public through a combination of email and social media marketing.

Maintain

I keep your website up to date, give you data about your visitors and their behavior, assist with your online marketing strategy, and provide regular backups of your site.
